Our Story

The Pet Haus is a family run pet salon with only one mission. To provide a safe, calm, and loving environment where pets receive the highest quality care.

We understand that grooming is not just about appearance — it's about hygiene, comfort, and the overall well-being of your pet. We provide a stress-free, loving and careful environment, using the highest quality products, professional grooming techniques, and personalized care to ensure each pet leaves happy, clean, and looking their best.
